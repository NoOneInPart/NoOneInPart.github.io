WHO WE ARE
Filmmakers For Foundations, Inc.
Based in Fremont, California

Filmmakers For Foundations is a new, California incorporated nonprofit organization focused on providing videography services to the aid of fellow nonprofits and organizations. We specialize in creating cinematic and promotional videos but have a wide range of video-making capabilities.

From a teaser video for the homepage of your website to an informational video that could promote a movement or event, to a documentary or film, if any of these ideas pique your interest or if you have any proposals of your own, feel free to reach out!
