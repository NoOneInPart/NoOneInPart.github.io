About us

Andrew Yuen
Founder/Director
email andrew.l.yuen@gmail.com
@Andrew CP
@cosmic.ay
Andrew Yuen is an aspiring filmmaker from Fremont, California. He is the vice-president at his high school's film club, as well as a videographer for his class's council and his school's ASB. Andrew has won a number of awards for his short films including the 2020 Reflections Award from the Peralta District (Alameda County) PTSA, as well as the 2021 WaterClips Videography Excellence Award from the Alameda County Water District. His films have been shown in numerous film festivals, and his videography works are showcased in his Instagram and YouTube channel.
