Al Donald Fast Food
 Hungry? Then why not come on down to our feature restaurant! We have the best tasting foods here, guaranteed to get you wanting more! So why not come down and get yourself some first-class chow!
Merry-Go-Round

 Are you bored of all the animals? Then why not try our state-of-the-art merry-go-round! It goes as fast as you want it, so that you can stop if you get dizzy. Additionally, it is human powered, so that you can be sure that you aren't ruining our great Earth!
Auditorium

 Need to take a break after that exciting merry-go-round ride! Then come on down to our brand new (as of 1995) auditorium! There are a variety of new and exciting shows, such as Hamlet and Six-hundred-and-sixty-six Shades of Red! All performed by our friendly staff here at the zoo.
Playground

 Need a quick workout while your kids are watching us feed the lions? Then come to our playground! There are lots of new brand-name workout equipment waiting to be used!
Grass Park

 Want to go on a very pleasant picnic? Then go to our grass park, right near the hippopotamuses! You can have a great lunch, AND watch the animals!
